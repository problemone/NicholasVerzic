    <h1 class="headerText">
    Dairy-vator
    </h1>
    <ul class="bodyText">
    <li>Led Mechanical Engineering Design course group project</li>
    <li>Addresses two primary problems:</li>
    <ul class="bodyText">
    <li>Milk cartons stuck at back of dairy fridge</li>
    <li>Stocking demands too much attention from employees</li>
    </ul>
    <li>Automated stocking system detects removal of carton and reacts by restocking product</li>
    <li>Rollers prevent milk sticking on slope</li>
    <li>Dynamic systems simulated for restocking trigger sensor calibration</li>
    </ul>
    <br>
    <p class="bodyText"><span class="darkBody">Platforms Used:</span> SolidWorks, Arduino</p>
    <p class="bodyText"><span class="darkBody">Tools Used:</span> Python, Matplotlib, NumPy, C++</p>
