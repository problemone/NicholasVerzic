<div class="fadeIn">
    <h1 class="headerText">
    Details
    </h1>
    <ul class="bodyText">
    <li>Led team developing robust pipeline to segment elevator control panel buttons and labels at pixel-level</li>
    <ul class="bodyText">
    <li>Manually segmented and annotated elevator buttons and labels, creating “The Living with Robots Elevator Button Dataset”</li>
    <li>Programmed augmentation algorithm and dataloader for image and segmentation data</li>
    <li>Designed vision transformer- and feature pyramid network model-based approaches to single-pass semantic segmentation of control panel features</li>
    <li>Introduced “recovery network” using class map generated from first pass output to identify potentially missed features</li>
    <li>Trained and evaluated all models on custom dataset</li>
    <li>Authored research paper published at 2024 IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS) titled “Recovering Missed Detections in an Elevator Button Segmentation Task”</li>
    <li>Gave Oral Presentation at IROS 2024</li>
    </ul>
    
    <li>Assisted PhD research on low-resolution video gaze tracking</li>
    <ul class="bodyText">
    <li>Wrote 3D spatial data loading and augmentation tools</li>
    <li>Helped develop projection algorithm from 3D point cloud to 2D data representation</li>
    <li>Produced code for interfacing between Open3D- and OpenCV-based processes</li>
    <li>Deployed tools for gaze tracking on lab machines</li>
    <li>Acknowledged in thesis titled “Cross-Domain Adaptation and Geometric Data Synthesis for Near-Eye to Remote Gaze Tracking”</li>
    </ul>
    </ul>
    <br>
    <p class="bodyText"><span class="darkBody">Platforms Used:</span> Linux</p>
    <p class="bodyText"><span class="darkBody">Tools Used:</span>Python, C++, PyTorch, NumPy, Detectron2, OCR-RCNN, VGG Image Annotator, Open3D, OpenCV, ROS</p>
