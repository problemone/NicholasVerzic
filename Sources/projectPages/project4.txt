    <h1 class="headerText">
    Details
    </h1>
    <ul class="bodyText">
    <li>Researched radio frequency ranging techniques for distributed sensor network to be deployed on celestial bodies</li>
    <li>Developed custom multilateration algorithms for localization of ad hoc sensor nodes launched from lander</li>
    <li>Implemented various optimization techniques to minimize uncertainty in predicted location, improving precision by 50%</li>
    <li>Collaborated with team on collapsible fin design</li>
    <li>Designed and conducted on-site experiments in simulated lunar surface environment</li>
    <li>Co-authored research paper published at 2020 IEEE Aerospace Conference titled “PHALANX: Expendable Projectile Sensor Networks for Planetary Exploration”</li>
    <li>Presented work at 2 oral presentations and 2 poster symposia</li>
    </ul>
